About Us

PPL Cricket Turf: The PPL Brotherhood

Post-COVID, mid-2022, cricket lovers stranded at home, missing the smack of the ball and the banter that makes every match unforgettable. That’s when Gaurav Palhekar (aka GP Bhai)had an idea—why not bring the gang back together and create a tournament where fierce competition meets unbreakable brotherhood? And just like that, PPL was born— the ultimate battleground for cricketers from all walks of life, all ages, and all skill levels. Fast forward three years, 13 seasons down, and we’re gearing up for PPL Season 14 -bigger, bolder, and packed with even more nail-biting rivalries. PPL has earned the title of “IPL of Pune”, where six teams battle it out, each led by a powerhouse owner, fighting for glory in the most intense auction-driven player selection. But PPL is more than just a league—it’s a family, built on competition, camaraderie, and some hilariously questionable cricketing decisions. Behind the scenes, a dedicated Committeeensures each season is more thrilling than the last. And if you think the drama and heroics are reserved for the turf, think again! Our official YouTube channel, “turf_ppl,”capturesevery epic moment— from jaw-dropping catches to post-match leg-pulling sessions. So, if you’re ready to witness grit, passion, and the kind of cricket banter that could start (and settle) wars,you’re in the right place.Welcome to the PPL Brotherhood—where the spirit of cricket is alive, well, and occasionally hilariously competitive! 🏏🔥
